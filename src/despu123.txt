The programmes offered by the school are designed to ignite passion for exploration, equipping learners with the analytical skills and scientific mindset. With dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a collaborative learning environment, the school offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Computer Science, Data Science, Industrial Maths, Computer Applications, Statistics, Mathematics, Environmental Science, Geology, Biochemistry, Organic Chemistry, Analytic Chemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Physics and Zoology.
